## Email Support

### Thread-Based Email Management

ReplAInow includes a complete email helpdesk with Gmail-style threading, rich text editing, and powerful AI assistance.

**Dashboard Views:**
- **Inbox** - All email threads
- **Unassigned** - Threads not yet assigned to an agent (action required)
- **My Tickets** - Your personal queue
- **Review Queue** - AI responses that need verification before sending
- **Auto-Sent** - Quality monitoring view for AI-sent emails

### How AI Handles Email

When a customer sends an email, the AI reads it, understands the intent, and decides on the best approach:

**Scenario 1: AI Can Fully Resolve**
Simple questions get instant, accurate answers. The AI responds immediately with information from your shop data. Example: "What's your return policy?" â†’ AI quotes your actual Shopify policy.

**Scenario 2: AI Collects Missing Information**
If a customer wants a refund but didn't provide their order number, the AI asks targeted questions to gather everything needed before handing off to your team. Example: "I'd be happy to help with your refund! What's your order number?" This ensures your team gets complete information instead of playing email ping-pong.

**Scenario 3: All Info Collected, Agent Must Act**
Once the AI has everything needed (order number, customer preference, photos of damage, etc.), it writes a professional handoff email confirming all details and sets realistic expectations. Your agent sees a complete summary and knows exactly what to do.

### AI Quality Scoring

Every AI response gets a quality score (0-100) across five dimensions: response quality, context coverage, information accuracy, language fluency, and overall confidence.

**Auto-Send Configuration:**
- **90-100 (Excellent)** - Auto-sent automatically
- **80-89 (Good)** - Auto-sent if threshold set to 80 or lower
- **70-79 (Fair)** - Sent to Review Queue for agent verification
- **Below 70** - Always requires agent rewrite

You control the threshold. Set it to 90 for conservative (fewer auto-sends, more human review), or 80 for balanced (more automation, faster responses).

### Email Features for Agents

**Rich Text Editor:**
- Full HTML formatting (bold, italic, lists, links)
- Attachments up to 25 MB per file
- CC and BCC fields for professional email management
- Draft auto-save every 10 seconds

**AI Draft Handling:**
When AI creates a draft response, agents can:
- Send as-is (one click)
- Edit before sending (use AI as starting point)
- Discard and write fresh response

**Order Management Integration:**
If a customer mentions an order number, ReplAInow automatically loads the full order details in the sidebar. Agents can:
- View order products, shipping address, and tracking
- Process refunds directly from the dashboard
- Cancel orders or line items
- Update shipping addresses

**Information Collection Intelligence:**
The AI extracts phone numbers, callback times, and order numbers from emails (context-awareâ€”it knows if "12345" is a phone number or order number based on conversation flow). Agents see collected information highlighted in the sidebar, ready to use.

## Settings & Configuration

### Team Management

**Four Role Types:**
- **Owner** - Full access to everything
- **Admin** - Same as Owner except can't delete the shop
- **Agent** - Handle conversations, use tools, reply to customers (cannot change shop settings)
- **Viewer** - Read-only access for managers or trainees

**Easy Onboarding:**
Invite team members by email, assign their role, and share the generated invite link. They click, create their account, and join your team. Each agent sets up their profile with photo, name, and signature for professional customer interactions.

### AI Configuration

Customize your AI assistant's identity:
- AI name (default: "ReplAI" or use your own like "Sophie")
- AI avatar/photo
- AI greeting message
- Response personality (friendly, professional, casual)

### Email Settings

**Auto-Send Control:**
- Enable or disable AI auto-sending entirely
- Set quality threshold (50-95 scale)
- Configure email template colors and branding
- Customize AI agent name for emails
- Set email closing signature

**Mailgun Integration:**
Email sending is handled automatically through Mailgun. You get a dedicated sending address, or configure a custom domain (support@yourshop.com) for branded emails.

### General Settings

- **Language Selection** - Set dashboard and widget language (auto-detected from Shopify)
- **Timezone Configuration** - All timestamps show in your local timezone
- **Widget Logo** - Upload your shop logo for the chat widget header
- **Widget Installation** - One-click link to Shopify Theme Editor to activate the widget
