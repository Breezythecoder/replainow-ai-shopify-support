## üé® Visual Identity

### Color Strategy
```
Primary: Purple (#6B46C1) - Brand recognition
Action: Green (#10B981) - CTAs & success
Base: Grays - Content hierarchy
Accent: White - Premium feel
```

### Typography
```
Headlines: Inter Bold (56px hero, 48px sections)
Body: Inter Regular (18px optimal reading)
Small: Inter Regular (14px metadata)
```

### Design Elements
- **Gradients**: Used sparingly for CTAs
- **Shadows**: Subtle depth on cards
- **Borders**: Light gray for separation
- **Radius**: 8-16px for modern feel
